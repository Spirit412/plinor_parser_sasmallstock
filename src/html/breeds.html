ABOUT US
The purpose of SASmallstock.com is to provide users with a functional and efficient search and comparison facility for smallstock animals based on genetic evaluations. Genetic merit is based on the South African genetic evaluation and the information and data supplied through SA Stud Book's genetic services. Registered South African Beef Breeders have access to the information of their own herds' female animals as recorded on Logix through the SASmallstock.com mating facility.

The mating facility and inbreeding calculations of SASmallstock.com is a complete solution to matings and breeding goal achievement for smallstock breeders participating in Logix Beef. The mating facility is available to licensed users and is accessed with a user's name and password.
